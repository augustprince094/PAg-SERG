About me

Prince has over five years of experience using process intelligence techniques to provide sustainable solutions in the agrifood sector. He is passionate and inspired by the extent to which innovative solutions driven by model-based techniques and life cycle asessment can drive, transformation and accelerate sustainability within the agri-food and animal feed industry.

To this end, Prince aspires to support current efforts to create a more efficient, sustainable, and circular agrifood system. This desire has been the foundation for his current research in collaboration with Jefo Nutrition Incorporated to leverage biotechnology to drive sustainable livestock production.

Research interest

Environmental product declaration

System dynamic-based integrated assessment

Integrating system dynamic models of social, economic, and environmental earth systems and their interdependencies to design solutions for net-zero emission targets.

Circular bioeconomy

Adopting the butterfly philosophy to recycle and circulate biproducts as long as possible

Upcycling and upscaling systems design

Unlocking potential transformation and utilization of waste and co/by-products from primary production systems into value added products.

Decision analsysis and support systems

Integrating model-based and deep learning oriented approaches into digital solutions to drive sustainable healthy choices

Environmental-Nutrition Analysis

Biobased polymers

Decision Analysis
